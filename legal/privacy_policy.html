    If and to the extent WOOBLE is processing your personal data based on WOOBLE's Legitimate Interest, specifically where WOOBLE pursues its legitimate interest to engage in direct marketing or to apply profiling in relation to direct marketing, you have the right to object to such a use of your personal data at any time.     Wherever WOOBLE is processing your personal data based on your consent, you may at any time withdraw your consent by unsubscribing or giving us respective notice of withdrawal (dataprotectionofficer@wooble.org). In case WOOBLE is required to retain your personal data for legal reasons your personal data will be restricted from further processing and only retained for the term required by law. However, any withdrawal has no effect on past processing of personal data by WOOBLE up to the point in time of your withdrawal.     If you take the view that WOOBLE is not processing your personal data in accordance with the requirements in this privacy statement or under applicable data protection laws, you can at any time, to the extent required by applicable law, lodge a complaint with your locally relevant data protection authority, specifically when you are located in an EEA country, or with the data protection authority of the country or state where WOOBLE has its registered seat.
