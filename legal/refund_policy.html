CANCELLATION AND REFUND POLICY

Last Updated: 23rd January, 2023


This Agreement shall continue until You cancel Your subscription or until terminated by WOOBLE.

How to cancel Your WOOBLE subscription?

  1. Log in to Your account at wooble.org
  2. Go to the Profile->Payments page
  3. Click the Cancel Subscription link at the bottom of the page
  4. Click Yes, Continue
  5. Choose a reason for cancellation and click Cancel Subscription

Refunds

You may cancel Your subscription at any time on Your account page; however, there are no refunds for cancellation. Unless you cancel your subscription before your billing date, you authorize WOOBLE to charge the subscription fee for the next billing cycle.

If You cancel before the end of Your Subscription Term You will have access to Your account for the remainder of the Subscription Term. In the event that WOOBLE suspends or terminates Your account for Your breach of these terms, You acknowledge and agree that You will receive no refund or exchange for any unused time on a Subscription, any Subscription Fees for any portion of the services, any content associated with Your account, or for anything else.
