Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible to apply for the Wooble Legacy Scholarship Program?

The Wooble Legacy Scholarship Program is open to students and young professionals from all over the world who are looking to showcase their work and expand their digital presence. We are looking for individuals who are passionate about their field, have a creative spirit, and are eager to make their mark on the digital world.

What is the application process?

The application process is simple. Click on the "Apply Now" button and fill out the application form. You will be asked to provide some personal information, answer a few short questions about your goals and why you are interested in the scholarship, and submit examples of your work if available. Once your application is submitted, it will be reviewed by our selection committee.

What is the deadline for applications?

The application deadline for the Wooble Legacy Scholarship Program is 9th June 2023. We encourage all applicants to submit their forms well in advance of the deadline to ensure they have plenty of time to complete the application process.

When will I find out if I've been selected?

Successful applicants will be notified via email by 9th June 2023. If you have not heard from us by this date, please assume that your application was not successful this time around. However, we encourage you to apply again in the future!
