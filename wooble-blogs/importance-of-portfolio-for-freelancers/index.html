IMPORTANCE OF PORTFOLIO FOR FREELANCERS

- Santanu Mishra Jena on 17th July 2022


In this age of digitalisation, it is important for us to strengthen our digital footprint if we want to redefine our careers. For a freelancer, it is quite important to update their digital presence which will make them stand apart from others.

A portfolio is a tangible proof that you can display to the hiring manager what you have done and what you can do. Your Career portfolio is evidence that elaborates the ability of what you are offering, presented in a specialized format to showcase the highlights of your works. Being a freelancer, it is important for you to be able to tell your potential clients what you are capable of by showing them proof of your work. So, an impactful portfolio is an absolute necessity for a freelancer. The upper hand a freelancer can get from a well-designed or curated portfolio is as follows:

  • A Strong Portfolio will increase your chance of landing a good job as a freelancer.
  • A portfolio is also a method of self-discovery and confidence building that will boost your career in the long run.
  • A well-prepared portfolio also provides opportunities for freelancers to provide their work history in one place in order to promote awareness and reputation of their work.
  • An impactful portfolio also set one freelancer apart from another freelancer in a professional and academic setting.
  • A well-made portfolio will show potential clients your legitimacy and the level of professionalism you stick to and will also display your character.
  • In a nutshell, a professional portfolio is a type of marketing tool. With a comprehensive and impactful portfolio, a freelancer can easily showcase his/her best projects, previous and current clients, and also what he/she can offer to a company.
