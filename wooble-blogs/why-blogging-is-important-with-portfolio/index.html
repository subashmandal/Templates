WHY BLOGGING IS
IMPORTANT WITH PORTFOLIO

- Arpita Gupta on 12th October 2022


Nowadays adding a blog to a portfolio has become a status quo. Blog in your portfolio will connect you with like-minded individuals if you want to attract clients, if you want to get noticed at all then you should include a blog in your portfolio. The blog should be updated regularly, it should also make your followers part of your journey. Blogs display the most relevant content on top. Projects are displayed in chronological order, as a result, the first project that your visitors will see is the one that represents your current skills. But the blog will display the relevant content on which you have worked hard. So, adding a blog to our portfolio website is very essential. Some of the additional features that the blog provides are as follows:

Visitors like to engage with blogs:

A big difference between simple portfolios and portfolios with blogs is the level of audience engagement. Portfolio visitors will just take a look at your portfolio and will leave. To make your portfolio more interesting, you should add a blog to it which will increase the audience engagement rate. Visitors will comment on the blog entries and can share your blog posts or can suggest you some topic for which they want a blog

Blogs Reflect your personality:

Portfolio showcase your skills and abilities. But a blog will showcase or reflect your personality. People will follow your blog because they appreciate your content. Recruiters will think you would be the right person for their company.

Blogs are content-driven, and portfolios are design-driven:

Portfolios will demonstrate your professional journey through an impactful design. But Portfolios with blogs not only showcase the design but also display the content which will summarize your professional career. Some of your clients or visitors can relate to the experiences you have shared in your portfolio blog which will be beneficial for you in the long run.
