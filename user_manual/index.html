New to Wooble? In this tutorial, we'll show you what to expect — and do — when you first sign up for Wooble.
GETTING STARTED:
What is wooble?

Wooble is a one stop platform which provides the user a domain to create and customize their own digital portfolio.

A variety of templates are available for users to choose from in order to showcase and enhance their personal or professional interests. They can incorporate a range of multimedia files such as videos, audios, digital designs, PDFs, certificates, blogs, and other file formats to enrich their portfolio. This portfolio can be easily accessed from any location as and when needed.

Wooble's versatile portfolio platform has everything you need to showcase your portfolio with a whole web presence which is easy to get accessible by anyone and everyone, it also saves time and money.

Wooble offers 10+ mystic, pixel perfect, web and mobile friendly customized templates to fit every style of the profession.

Getting started with

Wooble is an all-in-one platform that enables ingenious professionals to create and maintain their portfolios and blogs without the need for coding expertise.

First enter your initials, choose your profession and then choose majestic picture you want to follow up after this you are signed in successfully and then you will get directed to the home page.

Sign up for free to start building your first portfolio get started with the free trial to build your first portfolio.

Log in to Wooble

Home page

Once you get started and successfully signed in, you will be directed to the homepage.

You can start with describing about yourself and then upload your banner image and can also mention your social media links for easy accessibility in the future.

Here onwards, you can start with your portfolio

Gallery section

The gallery section is where you can showcase your artistic vision with a collection of curated images that capture your essence and journey.

You can upload your favorite pictures, add captions or quote, and organize them in your portfolio.

You can edit and delete your pictures.

Work section

The work section is where you can present your work with precision and showcase your passion for excellence and dedication to delivering exceptional results in any field you choose.

You can include crucial assignments, projects, documents, and journals, freeing yourself from the need for frequent paper usage. This way, you can access your work anytime and keep it organized in one place.

You can edit and delete your work.

Blog section

The blog section offers a glimpse into your creative process and a space for you to share your thoughts, inspirations, and experiences, providing a behind-the-scenes look at the heart and soul of your work.

If you upgrade to premium you will get access to blog sharing and blog reading.

You can edit and delete your blog.

More section
Design -

The design page of your portfolio is where creativity blends with functionality.

You have the freedom to choose from over 10+ templates and customize the styles and layout of your portfolio to fit your unique preferences. With a range of uniquely designed templates option, you can elevate the standards of your portfolio and make it stand out.

Resume -

The resume page of your portfolio will encapsulate your experiences, achievements and skills into a compelling narrative that highlights the ability to make a lasting impact in every industry you want to work in.

In the resume section you can upload your professional resume for every opportunity.

You can edit or delete your resume.

Video resume -

This feature enables users to upload and share their video resume through their digital portfolio, increasing their chances of landing job opportunities by showcasing their skills, experience, and personality in an engaging format.

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) -

This feature enables users to optimize their portfolio by utilizing either their own chosen keywords or by incorporating the suggested keywords provided by this feature.

Analytics -

This feature enables the user to access the statistics of viewership of the portfolio, the analytics gives you the sectional view report of users portfolio along with the overall view report.

Email signature -

Having an email signature adds a level of professionalism and legitimacy to an individual's portfolio, we provide a cohesive and consistent digital image. This not only helps with branding efforts, but also improves traffic generation and increases the likelihood of receiving a higher response rate. By including a well-designed email signature, individuals can effectively communicate their brand identity and create a lasting impression with potential clients or employers.

  • There is a standard email signature for free users with Wooble watermark.
  • Digital email signature for using royal subscription plan.
Custom domain -

By utilizing our custom domain, individuals can create a personalized portfolio that represents your unique identity and brand.

  • Users can adopt our custom domain name, which is "wooble", to establish a distinct online presence and showcase their work to the world.
  • Users have the option to purchase a custom domain that is fully personalized to their own identity and brand.

This provides a professional and personalized persona that sets them apart and enables them to leave a lasting impression on potential clients or employers.

Audible blogs -

By offering audible blogs, individuals can now listen to their favourite blogs and articles, allowing them to multitask and be productive while on the go. This saves time and effort by eliminating the need to sit down and read lengthy articles, and provides the convenience of being able to consume content audibly, anywhere and anytime.

Magic contact sharing -

With magic contact sharing, users can create their own digital business card, which can be easily shared through NFC and QR technology or by sharing a link.

This enables others to access the user's information, making it convenient for networking and communication.

Blog sharing -

Wooble's cutting-edge blog sharing technology offers users a seamless and intuitive experience, allowing for efficient and effective distribution of content across various platforms. With its advanced features and user-friendly interface, Wooble provides individuals with the tools they need to connect with their audiences and establish a powerful online presence.

Password protected pages -

This feature enables you to protect your portfolio, you can protect your portfolio in the following ways

  • Work section- once you enable work section, you can generate the password and the work section will get protected, if users share their work section then user has to share the password also to give access to their work section
  • Blog section- once you enable blogs section, vou can generate the password and the blog section will get protected, if users share blog section then user has to share the password also to give access to their work section
  • Gallery- once you enable gallery section, you can generate the password and the gallery section will get protected, if users share gallery then user has to share the password also to give access to their gallery section
  • Featured article- once you enable featured article section, you can generate the password and the featured article will get protected, if users share featured article section then user has to share the password also to give access to their work section.

At last if vou enable all the sections vou can password protect all the section.

Theme -

The dark and light theme of Wooble's portfolio is implemented using a triadic color scheme consisting of three colors. Which gives a relief and convenience to your eyesight.

Profile -

The profile section allows you to view the current status of your portfolio and update your personal information at any time.

Settings -

The setting section gives you the liberty to change the properties of your portfolio. Important actions can be controlled in the setting section.
